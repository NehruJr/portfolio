I build cross-platform apps end to end — from the Flutter UI down to the payment flow and the backend that holds it all together. My apps have handled electricity bills, stock trades, event tickets and personal training plans, which means I've had to get comfortable with a lot of different problems: real-time data, local payment gateways, offline storage, live location tracking.

What I care about most is the 1% that makes an app feel considered — the animation that responds instead of just plays, the empty state that actually helps, the checkout that doesn't make you nervous.
